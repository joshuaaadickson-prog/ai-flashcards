Built by AI, for AI Study

Welcome to AI Flashcards Studio. This project is a celebration of Human-AI Collaboration. While Antigravity AI handled the heavy lifting of code and design, the soul of the platform was shaped through a series of creative human sparks. It's the ultimate synergy: machine precision meeting human intuition.

🤝 Collaborative Authorship

From the glassmorphism UI to the underlying neural logic, this app was built in a live, interactive dialogue. AI wrote the characters, but a human provided the storyline. It's like "farm-to-table" code—high-quality ingredients, thoughtfully assembled for a premium result.

⚡ Pure-Context Generation

Our intelligence engine is strictly grounded. It forms questions ONLY from the information provided in your notes. We've optimized it to skip the noise and focus entirely on the material you choose to study.

⚖️ Strict & Fair Grading

When you answer, the AI grades your response strictly by what is in the notes. We've tuned the grading engine to recognize semantic synonyms while remaining disciplined enough to never demand facts you didn't supply.

How It Works

1. Input: Paste text or upload images/PDFs of your study material.
2. Analyze: Our AI "Brains" (Gemini or DeepSeek) scan your data in seconds.
3. Quiz: You get localized, high-density flashcards perfectly matched to your source.
4. Persist: Your keys and settings are saved locally for a private, on-demand tutor experience.
